**X Suns**

"If you're a fan of dark, winding instrumentals, do yourself a favor and put X Suns on your "to listen" list. The Seattle trio sit somewhere in the middle of the brutal intensity of Russian Circles and the firework-filled wall of sound of Explosions in the Sky. It's like shoegaze with a backbone. Start with the 11-minute "Pillars for Palaces." Lay back, close your eyes, allow it to envelop you and carry you away to sea." - Megan Seling (The Stranger)

**A Collective Sunconscious**

A Collective Subconscious is a Portland, OR–based instrumental rock band delivering massive, sound-bath–like sets that hit as hard as they mesmerize. Their sound shifts from explosive crescendos to hypnotic, meditative passages, blending the atmospheric depth of post-rock with the intricacy of math rock and the weight of doom. Formed in 2012, the band has released four full-length records using a an organic, flow-driven writing process inspired by nature, psychedelics, and the tension between old wisdom and modern living. A Collective Subconscious crafts sonic journeys that might feel more unearthed than composed, inviting listeners into a liminal space where the forest breathes, time bends, and the subconscious speaks. For fans of: Mogwai, Russian Circles, Godspeed You! Black Emperor

**Skyscape Paradise**

skyscape paradise is an Olympia electronic rock project by singer-songwriter and producer Kady Everpetal, combining shoegaze, post-rock, and electronic dance elements.
