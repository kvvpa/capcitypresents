MaRaK

Since 2011, this Pacific Northwest duo has been distilling the atmosphere of their rain-slicked
evergreen home into a formidable sonic identity. They have meticulously forged a sound that is
as dense as the forests that inspired it, occupying the liminal space between progressive
complexity, doom-laden weight, and the raw grit of alternative metal.

A Sonic Duality: Mellow Meets Metal

Their music is a study in contrasts—a sonic representation of the region’s shifting weather. The
"mellow" side of their sound manifests in haunting, reverb-drenched passages and intricate, clean melodies that echo the stillness of a fog-covered morning.

However, this tranquility is frequently shattered by the "metal" side of their persona. By blending the crushing, down-tuned tempos of traditional Doom with the unconventional structures of Progressive Metal, they create a listening experience that feels both cerebral and visceral. It isn't just loud; it’s a deliberate, heavy movement that pushes against the boundaries of standard alternative metal.

Double B

Double B is an Olympia, Washington–based hip-hop artist known for sharp lyricism, commanding live performances, and a unique brand of humor. His music combines the rawness of 2000s Underground Hip-Hop with modern production, catchy hooks, and emotional honesty. Double B has shared the stage with nationally known artists including Brother Ali, Dead Prez, and Lyrics Born. He is also the founder of The Bridge Music Project, a nonprofit that helps underserved and incarcerated youth develop their voices through songwriting. On his most recent album, Millennial Trash, Double B raps about walking his dog, spending money too fast, and the invasion of the advertisements, finding meaning, humor, and never-ending anxiety through sharp observations of modern life. @DoubleBraps on Instagram
