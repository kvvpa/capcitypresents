**Static Ghost**

Static Ghost performances are high-intensity, hardware-driven sets. Recently playing a SYNTHICIDE show in Brooklyn, New York with Andi and Semantix, and A CRY NOW\CRY LATER event in Seattle. He has shared stages with XTR HUMAN, Ultra Sunn, Dame Area, Sexual Purity, Dancing Plague, and, appearing at Verboden Festival (Vancouver BC), Sinth (Portland), and upcoming festivals in November 2025 and January 2026!

**Hexx Head**

Hexx Head is the collaboration of artists Allie Coppola and Mike Frazier. The Boston-based electronic duo is known for blending industrial, EBM, and noise. Since their debut release, Seabeds Cough, they’ve toured from east to west coast, delivering off-kilter rhythms fused with dance music’s beat-forward structure.
