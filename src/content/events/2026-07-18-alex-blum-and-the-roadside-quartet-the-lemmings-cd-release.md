**Alex Blum & The Roadside Quartet**

**The Lemmings**

Lemmings are a homegrown original rock band from Olympia. Formed in the summer of 2012, the band has spent the years since writing, refining, producing, and performing their music at local venues and events throughout the region.

Their unique sound blends influences from the early days of rock ’n’ roll with modern blues, country, and hard-driving rock. The result is a collection of well-crafted, catchy songs delivered with tight-knit intensity and passion — so crank it up.
