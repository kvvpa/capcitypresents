**Manic Planet**

Born of the fusion of members of two local bands, Manic Clump and Angry Blue Planet, Manic Planet is here to give you some big brain splodies from our rock (and roll).

**Mattress**

Mattress is a new alternative emo rock band from Seattle WA. They blend a sound of 90s and 2000s style layered guitar riffs, multi part harmonies with catchy hooks with groovy and intense drumming to create a sound they describe as "Death Pop". Their music focuses on the mortality of aging and life, nostalgic call backs to a better time and energetic live performances.

**Pullover**

Pullover is a 5-piece Alternative/Emo band from Seattle, WA. Forming in the Summer of 2025, they pull inspiration from their local & midwest roots to create an evolved sound. Pullover is releasing their debut EP this Summer. Pullover consists of Remy Vernhes, Michael Egan, John Gibson, Evan Hartung, and Ben Becker.
