**Great Comet**

Great Comet, the Americana-tinged indie rock project of songwriter Aaron New, has always been a study in motion, but with the release of the sonically expansive second album, Stranger Land, that motion has found a new direction—a deliberate journey through the spaces between. The album is a powerful document of life on the road, of experiencing lasting love alongside devastating loss, and finding a home in the hushed moments of a world seen through a van window. This newfound dynamic range is the core of Great Comet’s live show, where the band careens from anthemic, in-your-face rock to moments of pin-drop intimacy. Though the performance pushes into electrifying new territory, the project remains grounded in New’s Appalachian roots, blending a reverence for classic storytelling with a modern indie edge. Stranger Land is more than an album; it’s the title of an ongoing story, capturing a pivotal moment for a project defined by change and proving that for Great Comet, the destination is still unknown, but the path forward has never been more compelling.

**Reagan Leilani**

Raegan Leilani is a singer-songwriter whose music blends jazz with vintage pop, creating a sound that's both nostalgic and fresh. Her songs explore themes of identity, connection, and the longing for belonging, weaving heartfelt lyrics with intricate harmonies. Perfect for fans of timeless melodies with a modern twist, Raegan’s music invites you to dive deep and feel every note.

**Karen Laura Peters**

Karen Laura Peters (she/her) is a Tacoma/Seattle based singer-songwriter and cellist. With her airy and authentic voice, soulful cello lines, and sensual groove, Karen's music and performances are unique, spacious and expressive. Trained as a professional cellist at Oberlin Conservatory, Karen regularly records & performs with touring artists and performs often with her Seattle-based dreamy & sultry neo-soul band Queen of Hearts. She has performed regionally on stages including Tractor Tavern (Seattle), Madame Lou's (Seattle), Sunset Tavern (Seattle), McMenamins (Tacoma), and Alberta Street Pub (Portland). Her recent single release, Patterns, is a tender and persistent expression of self-love and processing inherited family patterns. Featuring her bandmates from Queen of Hearts, Patterns was recorded at Sage Arts Recording Studio north of Seattle. Karen is the Co-Founder of the WA based music organization, Momentum, which helps musicians feel inspired, connected and empowered to build momentum on their music journey.
