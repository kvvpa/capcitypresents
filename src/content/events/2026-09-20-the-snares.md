The Snares

The Snares are an American psych-rock outfit based in Sacramento, California. The band currently consists of Izzie Gomez (guitar, vocals), Lukas Brodie (drums), Danielle Judith (organ, vocals) and Julian Paz (bass). The Snares have a variety of influences that can be heard in their sound, including garage/psychedelic rock, pop, noise rock, and post-punk.
